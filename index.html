About New Life Center

Hi! We are New Life Orphanage Center located in Busoga, eastern Uganda. New Life Orphanage began in the year 2016 after an alarming rise in the number of highly vulnerable children in the society. Our sole purpose is to try restore meaning to the lives of children whose safety, well-being and development is at risk by bringing together contributions, donations and sponsoring their education. Join us and together we can make this globe a better place for them. God Bless You!
